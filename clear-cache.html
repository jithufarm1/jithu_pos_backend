🧹 Clear Valvoline POS Cache

Quick Fix Instructions:

  1. Click "Clear All Cache" button below
  2. Wait for success message
  3. Click "Open Fresh App" button
  4. You should see the Valvoline logo!

If logo still doesn't appear:

  1. Open Chrome DevTools (F12 or Cmd+Option+I)
  2. Go to "Application" tab
  3. Click "Service Workers" in left sidebar
  4. Click "Unregister" next to the service worker
  5. Click "Storage" in left sidebar
  6. Click "Clear site data" button
  7. Close DevTools and hard refresh (Cmd+Shift+R)
