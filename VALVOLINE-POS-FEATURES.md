# Valvoline POS - Complete Feature Set

## Overview

Valvoline Instant Oil Change stores provide comprehensive automotive maintenance services. This document outlines all the features that should be included in the POS system.

## 🚗 Core Services

### 1. Oil Change Services
- **Conventional Oil Change**
- **High Mileage Oil Change** (75k+ miles)
- **Synthetic Blend Oil Change**
- **Full Synthetic Oil Change**
- **MaxLife Oil Change**
- **NextGen Oil Change**
- Oil filter replacement included

### 2. Fluid Services
- **Transmission Fluid Exchange**
- **Coolant/Antifreeze Service**
- **Brake Fluid Exchange**
- **Power Steering Fluid Exchange**
- **Differential Fluid Service**
- **Transfer Case Fluid Service**
- **Windshield Washer Fluid Top-Off**

### 3. Filter Services
- **Engine Air Filter Replacement**
- **Cabin Air Filter Replacement**
- **Fuel Filter Replacement**

### 4. Battery Services
- **Battery Replacement**
- **Battery Testing**
- **Battery Terminal Cleaning**

### 5. Wiper Services
- **Wiper Blade Replacement**
- **Wiper Blade Inspection**

### 6. Light Services
- **Headlight Replacement**
- **Taillight Replacement**
- **Turn Signal Replacement**
- **License Plate Light Replacement**

### 7. Tire Services
- **Tire Pressure Check**
- **Tire Rotation** (if applicable)
- **Tire Inspection**

## 🎨 UI/UX Principles

- **Fast**: Quick access to common tasks
